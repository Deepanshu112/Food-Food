Details about the dish

Do try this delicious Chicken Biryani recipe and enjoy it with your loved ones. When thinking of tandoor-style dishes, fish is not always the first thing that comes to mind. What the cooking process does for other meats works just as well for fish, imparting flavor and spice in equal measure.
