Details about the dish

dal soup recipe | dahl soup | lentil soup recipe | daal soup with step by step photo and video recipe. dal or lentil is one of an integral part of indian cuisine and is used for different types of recipes. generally, these recipes are gravy or curry-based recipes, which is generally served with choice of rice or roti recipes. however, there are other types of recipes too and soup is one such variant where different types of lentil is mixed together to form dal soup recipe. as i was explaining, the dahl soup is a versatile recipe and can be made with all the available types of lentils. in this post, i have used the mixed lentil to prepare the soup base but individual lentil also makes a great choice. particularly, the moong dal soup is my personal favourite and i make it with a hint of dill leaves in it. i make it often whenever i feel like to have something lite meal or have a sore throat problem. having said that, i have made a different choice with this recipe post. i have chosen mixed dal which makes it a perfect and balanced meal. it has all the nutrients from all the lentils used. moreover, it is protein-packed and hence can be served for lunch or dinner.
